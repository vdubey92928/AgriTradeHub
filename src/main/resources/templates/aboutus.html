About Us

Building a trusted digital marketplace for farmers and merchants

Who We Are

Agritrade Hub is a platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and merchants. Our goal is to simplify agricultural trading, ensure transparency, and empower farmers by giving them direct access to markets. We believe in building a sustainable agricultural ecosystem where everyone benefits.

With technology-driven solutions, we are transforming how agricultural products are bought and sold, making the process faster, fairer, and more efficient.

About the Client

Hukmawati Cold Storage Private Limited

Based in Barabanki, Uttar Pradesh, Hukmawati Cold Storage is a leading agricultural storage provider. The company offers state-of-the-art cold storage facilities to support farmers in preserving their produce and reducing post-harvest losses.

Their commitment to modernizing agri-logistics aligns with the goals of the Agritrade Hub project, making them an ideal implementation partner for empowering farmers and building sustainable food systems.
